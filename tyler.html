Rhetorical Analysis of a Written Text

My revised Rhetorical Analysis

We were asked to do the following: Conduct a formal rhetorical analysis of a written text. Your analysis should make and support a claim about how the text works to persuade its audience, based on its rhetorical features. Your claim should be supported by direct evidence (in the form of quotes) from the text, as well as reasoning, logic, and even speculation. Research about the context of the text is usually necessary, though how much depends. Your analysis should be well introduced and have a clear conclusion.
